Frequently Asked Questions

How do we access carbon credit revenue?

+
Rupiya manages the entire process: verification (MRV), documentation, and sale of credits on your behalf, sharing the revenue directly with your FPO and members.

What is included in the 'Training Support'?

+
We provide on-ground and digital training on Good Agricultural Practices (GAPs), residue-free protocols, digital literacy, and market quality standards.

How does the buy-back solution work?

+
We provide a formal buy-back agreement for all produce that meets our pre-defined quality standards, guaranteeing you a premium market.

What technology platform do you provide?

+
You get access to our FPO Dashboard to manage members, track farm activities, aggregate produce, and manage payments, all in one place.
