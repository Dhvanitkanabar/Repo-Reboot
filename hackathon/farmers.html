How We Increase Your Yield & Reduce Costs

Our proven 4-point system helps you shift to residue-free farming successfully.

Expert Advisory (App + On-Ground)

Get daily advice from our agronomists on the app, plus regular on-farm visits to solve problems fast. This guidance helps you use inputs correctly and save money.

Residue-Free Inputs

You get a complete kit of 35+ certified bio-inputs. These are safer for your soil, improve quality, and reduce your dependence on costly chemical pesticides.

AI-Powered Soil & Crop Health

We use satellite data to test your soil (no-cost) and AI to monitor your crop's health 24/7. This helps you apply the *right* input at the *right* time, increasing yield.

Full Risk Protection

We protect you with parametric insurance (for weather) and a guaranteed buy-back program for your qualified produce, ensuring your hard work pays off.
