Frequently Asked Questions

What certifications can you provide?

+
We can provide produce that is NPOP, NOP, GlobalG.A.P., and Fairtrade certified, based on your specific requirements.

How do you guarantee traceability?

+
Our technology platform captures data from the farm level (plot mapping, inputs used) all the way through aggregation, processing, and final shipment.

Can you handle large-volume, multi-location contracts?

+
Yes. Our strength lies in our large, distributed network of over 200 FPOs, allowing us to manage large, consistent supply contracts across India.

What is the typical lead time?

+
Lead time depends on the crop, required certifications, and volume. For spot buys, we can ship from our centers immediately. For contract farming, we align with the crop calendar.
